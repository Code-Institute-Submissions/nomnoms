NomNoms

Ingredients

  • Plenty of Flour
  • Lukewarm Water (250 ml for 2)
  • 1 teaspoon of Yeast
  • Salt

Pizza Dough

  • Put the water in a mixing bowl and add the salt. Mix with fingers until salt is disovled. Add a small amount of flour and mix again. Add the yeast and mix. Let stand for a few minutes
  • Once you have left the mixture alone for a few minutes, start adding more flour. You can add a good bit at a time. Mix with your hand until a dough is formed. Transfer to a surface and knead for 20 mins or more.
  • You'll know when the dough is ready as it will still be slightly tacky to touch but will no longer want to stick to your kneading surface.
  • Add some flour to the mixing bowl, lightly cover the dough in flour and transfer to the mixing bowl. Cover with a damp tea towel and leave to proove for a minimum of 2 hrs
